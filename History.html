While we haven't been open long, we hope to establish a deep and lasting connection within the Rock Hill, Maryland community. Mark and Nick Want to make this a place that is around not just for a few years but for possible generations to come. They don't think of this place as just another restaurant, but as a future staple of the community.

They plan to grow with this community through joining in the rich heritage, local community group involvement and the betterment of the surrounding area.
